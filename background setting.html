A group of geese have built a spaceship to explore space, but ducks have infiltrated the ship with plans to sabotage it. Geese must complete tasks on the map in the form of mini-games, including maintenance work on crucial systems. Ducks,disguised as geese, receive fake task lists to blend in, can identify other ducks, and have abilities to sabotage ship systems, travel through vents, and eliminate geese. For the geese to win, they must complete all tasks or identify and vote out all the ducks. Ducks win by reducing the number of geese to equal or fewer than the ducks, or by triggering a successful sabotage countdown.

Some content is sourced from the internet and the game Goose Goose Duck and is for reference only.
