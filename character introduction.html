After starting the game, players are randomly assigned to the Goose faction, Duck faction, or Neutral faction based on the number of players. Each faction has unique roles with different abilities.

Below are all roles, their abilities, and relevant notes.

Goose Faction
Role Skill Notes
Goose None You are immune to assassination by Assassins. Complete tasks, stay alive, and vote out the Ducks.
Juicy [Goose] Completing tasks increases your bounty, survive to the end; Killers can only eliminate you when alone. You are safe from Assassins. After completing a task, a money bag symbol appears by your name in the Ducks’ view.
Psychic [Goose] Allows you to know the number of ghosts. None
Vigilante [Goose] You can kill one bird per game; use it wisely. Only one kill per game, with no consequences.
Sheriff [Goose] Can kill anyone, but killing a fellow Goose results in your death. Killing neutrals or Ducks is safe; killing a Goose is lethal. Ensure accuracy before attacking.
Canadian Goose [Goose] Your killer auto-reports the kill after one second. Professional Killers and Bombers don’t trigger reports. Pelicans eating you will also auto-report after one second.
Lover [Goose or Duck] Survive with your lover to the end; if one dies, so does the other. Lovers can be Goose-Goose or Goose-Duck. Your lover’s name appears in pink.
Engineer [Goose] Your mini-map estimates the location of live Ducks starting sabotage.
You can use the vent system briefly.		 Hide in specific objects for safety and pinpoint Duck locations.
Mimic [Goose] Ducks see you as one of their own. Use observation and communication to provoke suspects, identifying Ducks.
Detective [Goose] Investigate players to see if they killed in the current round. Killers show a devil icon; innocents show a white Goose icon. Note: killers aren’t always Ducks.
Birdwatcher [Goose] See through walls but have limited vision. Skill deactivates in darkness.
Bodyguard [Goose] Earn rewards for successfully protecting a designated player; sacrifice yourself if needed. None
Politician [Goose] Survives tie votes and cannot be jailed. Only available on maps with jails.
Locksmith [Goose] Can open the jail door anytime. Only available on maps with jails.
Undertaker [Goose] Investigate bodies to see their role. Hold Shift for three seconds to use. Identity revealed in meetings.
Influencer [Goose] Alerts all Geese upon your death. Neutrals and Ducks won’t receive alerts.
Adventurer [Goose] Immune to environmental hazards. Applies only to mummies, chandeliers, boulders, and drawbridges; Not locusts.
Avenger [Goose] Gain temporary killing power if you witness a murder. None
Astral Walker [Goose] Soul can teleport through walls. Body remains immobile, vision black-and-white, and can hear sounds.
Spy [Goose] Hide in corners or gaps. None
Technician [Goose] Mini-map estimates the starting location of sabotage. None
Tracker [Goose] Follow a player's movements. 技Arrow points to the tracked player
Disappears if they die or get eaten by Pelican.
Tracer [Goose] See who is outside during sandstorms. Only appears in Ancient Sands map.
Latchkey Kid [Goose] Can unlock locks from the inside. Only appears in Ancient Sands map.
Duck Faction
Role Skill Notes
Duck None None
Professional duck[Duck] Geese can’t see players you kill; corpses auto-report when approached. Killing a Canadian Goose won’t auto-report. Neutral and Ducks see your kills and may report.
Spy [Duck] Voting solo for a player reveals their role upon returning to the game. Discover identities and eliminate for strategic gain.
Silencer [Duck] Can mute players, preventing them from speaking or typing in meetings.
Cannot use vents or hide.		 None
Assassin [Duck] During voting, kill by guessing a role; a wrong guess results in your death. Two guesses per game, one per meeting.
Informant [Duck] Voting solo for a player sends them to jail. Only available on maps with jails.
Party [Duck] Change a player’s voice pitch during meetings. None
Bomber [Duck] Place bombs on players. Can’t kill directly, only through bombs, which may return to you.
Identity Thief [Duck] Morph into your recent kill before the meeting. Voice may reveal your identity.
Ninja [Duck] Kill two birds within range but has a threefold cooldown. Faster recovery in forbidden zones. Only appears in Jungle Temple map.
Mortician [Duck] Drag corpses slowly. None
Invisible [Duck] Turn invisible for 5 seconds. Can’t see players while invisible, but moves faster.
Serial Killer [Duck] Killing targets shortens cooldown; killing others lengthens it. Target marked by red arrow.
Cannibal [Duck] Eat a corpse once per game. Eating ability activates 3 seconds after a kill.
Shapeshifter [Duck] Transform into another player. Obtain transformation by “draining” a player; revert back after time limit. Voice may reveal you.
Mercenary [Duck] Gain rewards for eliminating specific players. Counters Bodyguard [Goose].
Warlock [Duck] Can summon a deadly swarm of locusts. Only appears on the Ancient Sands map. You cannot initiate sabotage tasks, but gain the ability to summon locusts by completing sabotage tasks. After summoning locusts, any players remaining in the locust-infested room after a set time will die instantly.
Psychic [Duck] Can remotely communicate with and kill players. After using the skill on a character, there is a 10-second delay. Then, your perspective shifts to that of the victim, allowing conversation, though you are immobile. After an additional 10 seconds, the victim dies.Killing a Canadian Goose results in an automatic report.
Neutral Faction
Role Skill Notes
Dopey Bird Win by being voted out. Make others suspicious of you.
Duelist Dopeys Get voted out after the other Dopey Bird dies; completing fake tasks enables the ability to kill them. Arrow points to other Dopey Bird’s location after task completion.
Vulture Win by eating a set number of corpses, based on player count. Blue arrow shows corpse location upon death.
Falcon Forces Goose wins via tasks and Duck wins via sabotage. Can kill players. “Falcon Time” initiates with only three players left, preventing meetings.
Pelican Swallow players, but they won’t die until a meeting is called. Swallowed players can communicate with the Pelican and other players in its stomach. The Pelican’s skill cooldown is determined by the number of players in its stomach. Once a meeting is called, all players inside the Pelican die instantly. If the Pelican is killed before a meeting, the players in its stomach are released. If the Pelican swallows a player carrying a bomb, the bomb explosion kills only the Pelican, sparing those inside. When only three players remain, including the Pelican, “Pelican Time” begins, preventing meetings. If time runs out, the Pelican wins. Swallowing a Canadian Goose results in an automatic report after one second.
Dove Win by infecting every player in a single round. The cooldown for your skill depends on the total player count. You can also use the vent system, sewers, or hide. After a meeting ends, the number of infected players resets, requiring you to reinfect everyone. Note: The Dove’s victory condition has the highest priority, meaning if all players are infected, the Dove wins instantly.
