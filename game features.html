Game features

After starting the game, players are divided into three factions: Geese, Ducks, and Neutral.

Faction Description
Geese Geese are the good guys, winning by completing tasks or voting out all Ducks and Neutral roles.
Ducks Ducks are the bad guys who gain sabotage abilities through completing sabotage tasks. Teammates’ names appear in red in the Ducks’ view. Ducks can perform Goose tasks, but this does not advance the Geese’s progress. Ducks win by killing the Geese or completing certain sabotage tasks.
Neutral Neutral roles have unique winning conditions based on their specific role.

When a Duck kills a player from the Goose faction, they experience a cooldown before being able to kill again, while Geese leave a corpse upon death.
When a Goose finds a corpse, they can press R to report it, triggering a meeting where players discuss who might be a Duck. After everyone speaks, players can vote to eject a player.

Additionally, each map has a public alarm point (marked by a small bell) where players can ring to call a meeting, though there are limitations and cooldowns per round.
