The game currently has eight maps.

Below are images and brief descriptions.

Basement

A large portion of the Basement is dark. These areas need careful observation as they are common locations for villains.

Goose Chapel

The layout of Goose Chapel is densely packed, making it ideal for Ducks to attack lone Geese. It’s best to complete tasks with companions.

Mallard Manor

Mallard Manor is connected through narrow hallways, and the entire map is brightly lit, making it more challenging for the villains to attack.

Nexus Colony

The map is divided into two sections, connected by teleporters for quick travel between them. However, avoid traveling alone.

Black Swan

Some rooms lack lighting, so remember them to prevent villain attacks. Multiple tasks are located on the bridge, but be cautious of environmental hazards.

Mothership

This map has a unique design, resembling a large goose.

Jungle Temple

Environmental hazards are common, so be cautious of the jungle’s dangers. Different areas have distinct scenery.

Ancient Sands

Random sandstorms reduce everyone’s vision and make names invisible, though indoor areas remain unaffected.
