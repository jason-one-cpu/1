Game Reviews

Undoubtedly, Goose Goose Duck was one of the most delightful surprises for players in 2022. As a culmination of Among Us and Werewolf, Goose Goose Duck introduces innovative rules that enrich its playability. Its improved social and matchmaking systems allow players to start games without needing to gather a large group of friends, making it easy to enjoy a variety of creative gameplay elements. Both beginners and seasoned players can enjoy a great gaming experience with Goose Goose Duck. (Review by 17173)

In terms of gameplay, Goose Goose Duck is similar to traditional Werewolf-style games, centered around player deception and strategy. However, Goose Goose Duck offers more gameplay variety and unpredictable elements than typical Werewolf games. Unlike traditional Werewolf games, eliminated players’ faction identities aren’t revealed, adding extra fun and diversity to the game. From faction-based gameplay to hidden voting mechanisms, it’s clear that the developers are continuously expanding content to enhance the game’s depth and variety. Players can explore the creative minds of others in one of the game’s biggest highlights—the Workshop, where ideas are shared widely. It’s safe to say that after two months of playing Goose Goose Duck, there may still be undiscovered aspects of the game. The unique skills tied to each player’s role allow even those with ordinary identities to find enjoyment and avoid simply being a passive participant. Each player can find their own joy in the game. (Review by Gamer’s Handbook)
