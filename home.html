"Goose Goose Duck" is a strategy casual game developed by Gaggle Studios, released on the Steam platform on October 4, 2021.
In the game, players take on the roles of members of the Goose faction, Duck faction, or Neutral faction, and engage in intense and thrilling deduction battles with other players to secure victory for their respective factions.

It is recommended to download the game on Steam and play with friends.
Additionally, note that Goose Goose Duck requires a VPN accelerator to play, so please ensure you have a working one ready before starting.
